BlueBottle

BlueBottle USV drones operate on energy available at sea. They require no fuel, no crew and no supplies. They are self-deploying and self-retrieving and can roam widely or kept on station virtually indefinitely. The first planned autonomous voyage is a circumnavigation of Lord Howe Island following a transit from mainland Australia.
